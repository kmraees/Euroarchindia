ARCHITECTURAL FACADE SYSYEMS INTERNATIONAL(AFSI)

Architectural Facade System International b.v. (AFSI) is a leading manufacturer in aluminium Façade Systems. The AFSI standard product range is Q-Premium panel/Q-Excellent panel/QBasic panel systems and AFSI manufacturers a wide range of Sun Control Systems. Again this product range does not exclude any requirement which can be manufactured.AFSI manufactures their façade systems, in aluminium, stainless steel, copper and zinc. The manufacturing process is done in State of the Art production facilities in the Netherlands. AFSI has combined European technology with efficient manufacturing techniques in order to serve the growing demand for high tech and made to measure quality. Our key quality is however our flexibility, we do not try and dictate facade details to architects and we always take a project requirement as the governing factor in what AFSI supplies.

MOEDING

Consequently, for years Moeding Keramikfassaden GmbH has been focused on well-founded, practice-oriented consulting. Competence and know-how are characteristic of our specialized consultants. We even offer our knowledge and our experience in the planning stages, and provide on-site support. Moeding Keramikfassaden GmbH is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Girnghuber GmbH.
